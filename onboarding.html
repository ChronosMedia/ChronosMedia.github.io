Role-Specific Quick Guides

Live Team (Streaming & Broadcast)

  • Arrive early: target 60‚Äì90 minutes before call time.
  • Pre-flight checks: power, cables, audio levels, camera framing, encoder/internet.
  • During the live: minimal chatter, monitor audio meters, watch for dropped frames.
  • Red button rule: coordinate changes through the lead only.
  • Wrap: confirm recording saved and handoff complete.

Photo Team (Photography)

  • Respect the moment: be present but unobtrusive.
  • Consistency: keep white balance stable when possible.
  • Shot list: confirm must-haves (leaders, key moments, groups, details).
  • File handling: don't delete originals; back up promptly; clear naming.
  • Delivery: follow the approved workflow for edits and exports.

Audio Team (Live Sound & Recording)

  • Clarity first: vocals must be intelligible.
  • Feedback prevention: gain staging and smart EQ.
  • Mic etiquette: placement matters; tape cables safely.
  • Record when possible: capture a safety recording if approved.
  • Coordinate: communicate with Live/Photo leads for cues.

Church Teams (Worship & Ministry Environments)

  • Reverence: move quietly and avoid distractions.
  • Privacy: don't capture private moments without approval.
  • Service flow: confirm cues (prayer, communion, altar calls).
  • Dress & demeanor: professional, modest, aligned with the church culture.
  • Kids & sensitive content: follow consent guidance.

Code of Conduct & Dress Guidelines

Code of Conduct

  • Professionalism: communicate calmly and respectfully.
  • Confidentiality: don't share schedules, footage, or client details without approval.
  • Safety: tape cables and keep walkways clear.
  • Respect property: handle gear carefully and report issues immediately.
  • Social media: do not post content without explicit permission.

Church-Specific Onboarding Insert

  • Check-in: connect with the church point-of-contact before unloading.
  • Soundcheck: confirm speaking mics, music inputs, and livestream feed levels.
  • Camera positions: avoid blocking aisles; minimize trip hazards.
  • Prayer/altar calls: reduce movement; frame respectfully.
  • Children/minors: follow consent requirements.
  • After service: confirm deliverables and thank staff.
